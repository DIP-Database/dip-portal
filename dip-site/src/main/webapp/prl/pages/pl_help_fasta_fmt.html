Two formats are accepted:

  • fasta format: The sequence is compossed of at least 2 lines. The first line is the header which must start with the symbol: '>'. All characters after this symbol and until the next newline character (\n) are considered a comment describing the sequence. All other lines are interpreted as containing 1-letter code amino acid sequence information. This is an example of a sequence in fasta format:

    > ribulose bisphosphate carboxylase small chain [Rubisco]
    MRVTQGCFSFLPDLTDDQIAAQIQYCLEKGWAVNIEFTDDPHPRNTYWEMW
    GLPMFDLRDAAGIMRELAECRKIYGDRYIRISAFDSSHGWESTRLSFIVNRPK
    TEPGFRLDRQETVGRNLRYSTRSYAAERPEGERYS

  • Plain sequence: A sequence of 1-letter codes for amino acids. Newline characters and spaces are ignored. For example:

    MRVTQGCFSFLPDLTDDQIAAQIQYCLEKGWAVNIEFTDDPHPRNTYWEMW
    GLPMFDLRDAAGIMRELAECRKIYGDRYIRISAFDSSHGWESTRLSFIVNRPK
    TEPGFRLDRQETVGRNLRYSTRSYAAERPEGERYS
