This refers to how deep in the network of functional linkages ProLinks will search for proteins related to the input query. That is, it indicates how far the linked proteins are from the query protein submitted by the user. This is illustrated in the figure below (click on the image to see a larger version). Distance 1 involves Links directly related (one edge away) to the query, and all other distances consist of indirect links (two or more edges away from the query protein).

If you are searching for paths of linkages connecting two proteins, the option 1+ refers to 2-edge paths where all nodes are interconnected forming a triangle motif.
