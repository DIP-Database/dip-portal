Currently we fully support:

   NCBI RefSeq (e.g. NP_414777, NP_417652)
   NCBI GIs (e.g. 16131075, 16128228)
   Locus Tag (e.g. Rv0304c, Rv0753c)

However we also recognize other types of IDs, if they were annotated in the GenBank files, but can't guarantee one for all proteins and all genomes. This is the list:

   GeneID (e.g. 946425, 945190)
   SwissProt (e.g. P0A7B5, P0A7L8)
   trEMBL (e.g. Q43946, O32265)
   EcoCyc (e.g. EG10768, G6085)
   EcoGene (e.g. EG50002, EG10998)
   PDB (e.g. 2NPO, 2AKO)
   ASAP (e.g. ABE-0010468, AEC-0000188)

We are working on extending the list of IDs we support.
